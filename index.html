What We're About

The OHC of NYC is in part a consortium for independent, community-run hackerspaces, but it is also an open consortium for members of those hackerspaces.

The primary aim is to build a space and time for people to meet and make friends through a structured framing of pitches/rants/lightning-talks about projects, curios, or anything else we find interesting.
